About Us

JECRC Foundation in association with Venture Catalysts is organizing KHOJ- Bharat Startup Yatra in presence of Startups, investors and ecosystem enablers of the JAIPUR entrepreneurial ecosystem. We would reach out to Jaipur and organize a half-day event for the startup community which will include programs like panel discussions, keynote sessions / workshops, startup pitching, and networking sessions. We will select a few potential startups from the city and support them in any way possible (mentoring / funding/ corporate connects etc). All the participating startups will also get technology credit benefits from our partners like AWS, MICROSOFT, AWS STARTUP RAMP, etc. We will support them through our different initiatives like mentoring, funding, corporate connects, etc. The overall objective of KHOJ is to find out young entrepreneurs and support them to grow, and strengthen the local startup ecosystem.

About Khoj

Taking a step ahead, Venture Catalysts presents KHOJ - Venture Catalysts Bharat Startup Yatra an initiative to promote the spirit of entrepreneurship in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities of India at the grassroots level and bring all the stakeholders of the regional startup's ecosystem to one platform and lead a healthy dialogue to nurture the entrepreneurial spirit in the region. The event celebrates the spirit of innovation and provides young entrepreneurs an opportunity to get their startups featured. It embraces the need to identify and nurture young and talented entrepreneurs. KHOJ has already reached 10+ cities across the country. Some of the highlights have been : 10+ cities 400 + startups pitches 50+ speakers 2500+ audience 50+ collaboration partners (including AWS, Microsoft,IVCA, and many more) Media coverage And a ton of networking KHOJ intends to reach 100+ cities by year-end and support 10,000 entrepreneurs in their entrepreneurial journey.
